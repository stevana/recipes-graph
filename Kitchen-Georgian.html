Lobio Ⓥ

(Georgian; dinner)
  • 400g kidney beans
  • 150g walnuts
  • 1 medium onions
  • (5 cloves of garlic)
  • 1 Tbsp Ajika
  • 1 1/2 tsp blue fenugreek
  • 1 tsp coriander (dried)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp bohnenkraut (savory)
  • 50 ml lemon juice
  1. Fry up onions
  2. Add crushed walnuts, toast for a bit
  3. Stir in Ajika and spices
  4. Add and crush up the beans
  5. Add 100 ml bean water
by anoymous

Khachapuri

(Georgian; side)
  • 770g flour
  • 130g milk
  • 130g water
  • 1 egg
  • yeast
  • 33 ml oil
  • salt
  • mozzarella
  • feta
  • riccotta
  • 2 eggs
  • parsley
  • butter
  1. Make a dough and shape it as a khachapuri boats
  2. Heat oven to 190°C
  3. Fill the boats with cheeses
  4. Bake 20 min
  5. Take out boats, crack an egg into them
  6. Bake for another 8-15 min depending on desired egg consistency
by anoymous

Khinkali

(Georgian; dinner)
  • 1.1 kg flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 450 ml of warm water
  • 400 g mushrooms (or 350 grams of ground beef and pork mix)
  • 200 g of water
  • 1 tsp ground coriander seed
  • 1-2 mild red chilies minced
  • 3 tbsp fresh tarragon
  • salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground caraway seed
  • 2 small onions optional
  1. mushroom use ground coriander seeds, and taragon
  2. meat uses caraway seeds, add water in 25 g batches to make it juicy
