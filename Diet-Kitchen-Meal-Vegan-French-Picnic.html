Tomato tart Ⓥ

(French; starter, side, picnic)
  • puff pastry
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 4 tbsp tomato sauce
  • 4 to 5 large tomatoes
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 5 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp herbes de Provence (or dried Italian herbs)
  • 5 tbsp bread crumbs
  1. 35 min, 200°C
