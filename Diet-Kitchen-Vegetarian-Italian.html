Mascarpone Amaretti mousse

(Italian; dessert)
  • 500g mascarpone
  • 200ml cream
  • 115g powderded sugar
  • 165g Amaretti coarsely crushed plus more for garnish
  • 33ml Amaretto
  1. Whip the cream.
  2. Mix mascarpone with powdered, crushed Amaretti and Amaretto.
  3. Carefully fold in the cream cream.
  4. Serve immediately or keep in the fridge until serving.
