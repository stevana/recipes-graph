Papaya salad

(Asian; lunch, dinner)
  • 1.5 cups of green papaya / kohlrabi, long shredded
  • 2 hot chilies
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 1.5 Tbsp palm sugar
  • 2 pods of yard-long beans
  • 4 cherry tomatoes
  • 1 carrot
  • 1.5 limes
  • 2 Tbsp fish sauce
  • Tamarind
  • (2 Tbsp dried shrips)
  • 2 Tbsp peanuts
  • Chinese cabbage
  • Cucumber
  1. Smash together garlic, chillies, sugar, and tamarind to make a paste.
  2. Add the papaya, beans and carrot (julienne), lime juice fish sauce. Smash that up until they are a bit translucent and soft.
  3. Add the cut-up tomatoes and peanuts, smash that up a bit more.
  4. If needed, adjust flavour with fish sauce and sugar. Should be tangy, sweet, spicy.
by Ramon
