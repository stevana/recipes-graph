Mascarpone Amaretti mousse

(Italian; dessert)
  • 500g mascarpone
  • 200ml cream
  • 115g powderded sugar
  • 165g Amaretti coarsely crushed plus more for garnish
  • 33ml Amaretto
  1. Whip the cream.
  2. Mix mascarpone with powdered, crushed Amaretti and Amaretto.
  3. Carefully fold in the cream cream.
  4. Serve immediately or keep in the fridge until serving.

Tiramisu

(Italian; dessert)
  • 15 g cacao
  • 100 g coffee
  • 60 g Kaluha
  • 20 g Cognac
  • 1 Tbsp vanilla extract
  • 4 eggs
  • 65 g sugar
  • 1 g salt
  • 500 g mascarpone
  • 200 g biscuits
  1. Brew the coffee and combine with cacao while piping hot
  2. Once cooled down, add the boozes and vanilla extract and set aside
  3. In separate metal/glass bowl, combine eggs with sugar and salt
  4. On a steam bath bring up the egg mixture to 71 degrees C
  5. Mix the egg mixture until fluffy, 5-10 min
  6. Fold in mascarpone
  7. Assemble by building layers of biscuits soaked in the coffee mixture interleaved with the egg and mascarepone mixture
  8. Chill in fridge for a couple of hours before serving
