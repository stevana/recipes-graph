Milk ramen Ⓥ

(Asian; lunch, dinner)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 20g of ginger
  • 2 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 Tbsp miso
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 Tbsp chili bean sauce
  • 2 tsp kombu powder
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 300ml soy milk
  • ramen noodles
  • green onions
  • sesame seeds
  1. Fry garlic, ginger and white parts of the green onion in the sesame oil
  2. Add the other ingreedients, except the milk
  3. Add milk and bring to boil
  4. Cook ramen separately and combine
  5. Serve with chopped up green onlions and sesame seeds as topping
