Tirokafteri

(Greek; side)
  • 400g feta cheese
  • 200g strained yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • 1/2 chili pepper
  • 1/3 clove of garlic
  • 50g olive oil
  1. Blend everything, except the feta cheese and yogurt
  2. Fold in crumbled feta cheese and the yogurt
